Company Profile

Incorporated in 2013 December, The company was started as a savings scheme for 10 people who used to contribute Kes.10,000 each every month.

The three directors took the task of growing the company in two directions:

  1. Training and consultancy.
  2. Lending.

These two tasks are made possible by the highly experienced directors as well as a team of professional staff.

Business Model

The company formally started the lending business in January 2015.
The major clients at that time were permanently employed bankers. We would and still do give them salary advances repayable in a maximum of 3 months depending on the amount. The mode of appraisal is what the pay slip can accommodate. Motor vehicle logbooks and other collaterals are taken depending on the amount being sought.

Mid-year 2015 the company had grown enough to have surplus and this saw the birth of the group business.

The members contribute on weekly basis a minimum of Kes.250. This money eventually forms the loan security fund (LSF).
